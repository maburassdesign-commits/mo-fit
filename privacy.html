Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

MO FIT respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how the application collects, uses, and protects health and fitness information obtained with your explicit permission.

Information We Collect

  • Step count
  • Distance
  • Calories burned
  • Workout records
  • Heart rate
  • Sleep data
  • Blood oxygen (SpO₂)
  • Body fat
  • Profile information (age, height, weight, gender)
  • Historical health data (up to one year)

How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used only to:

  • Track workouts and daily activity.
  • Generate personalized workout recommendations.
  • Provide AI-powered fitness coaching.
  • Display progress charts and statistics.
  • Calculate recovery and training insights.
  • Improve user experience.

Data Sharing

MO FIT does not sell, rent, or share your personal or health information with third parties.

Data Storage

Health data is processed only after user authorization and is used solely for fitness-related features inside MO FIT.

User Rights

Users may revoke Huawei Health authorization at any time through the Huawei Health authorization settings.

Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the MO FIT development team.
