# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 2026

## Introduction

MO FIT ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the application collects, processes, stores, and uses your information.

## Information We Collect

With your explicit permission, MO FIT may access the following data from Huawei Health:

- Step count
- Distance
- Calories
- Workout records
- Body fat
- Heart rate
- Sleep data
- Blood oxygen (SpO₂)
- Personal profile information (height, weight, age, gender)
- Historical fitness and health data

## How We Use Your Data

The collected data is used only to:

- Display fitness statistics
- Generate workout recommendations
- Track training progress
- Provide AI-powered coaching
- Create recovery and wellness insights
- Generate charts and long-term progress reports

## Data Sharing

MO FIT does not sell, rent, or share your personal health data with third parties.

## Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

## User Control

You may revoke Huawei Health authorization at any time through Huawei Health or your device settings.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be published on this page.
